Ebento Marketing

An "end-to-end" search engine marketing company. The client wanted to use the analogy of a bento box that contains a complete and varied meal to convey the all-in-one services of his marketing company.

Adley Wadley

A baby apparel company. The client wanted to use their daughter Adaline as the main model, so I felt the logo should have a feminine touch. As their apparel felt very minimalistic and airy, I went with a script font and a natural, ink-drawing feel for the logo.

Nerd Assassins

Nerd Assassins is a youth jiu-jitsu apparel company. The client wanted to convey the values of academic and physical discipline, as well as incorporate a medieval combat element. I knew this client was planning to use this logo in a variety mediums at varying sizes, so I chose simple, bold colors and made the design to be responsive for sharing on websites and social media platforms.

Zenzet

Zenzet is a cloud data storage startup based in China.

Claremont Ekklesia

Logo design for the Claremont Ekklesia, a Christian magazine that investigates the intersection of faith, science, philosophy, and the arts.

The Lock Ministry

The Lock Ministry is a Christian prisoner rehabilitation non-profit based in Brea, CA. The client wanted to use the concept of water locks to convey the transitional process of re-introducing ex-convicts back into society.

Kareo Success Summit 2019

Logo design for Kareo's annual user conference. The theme for 2019 was baseball related, involving teamwork and unity on our software platform.
